Day 1: Push - Shoulder Focus

  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 4 sets - 16, 12, 8, 16 reps
  • Butterfly Machine: 4 sets - 16, 12, 8, 16 reps (Last set: static hold in stretched position for 1 sec + 3 partial reps)
  • Incline Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 sets - 16, 12, 8 reps
  • Heavy Lateral Raises: 2 sets - 16, 12 reps
  • Seated Dumbbell Triceps Extension: 2 sets - 16, 12 reps
  • Cable Triceps Pushdown: 4 sets - 16, 12, 8, 16 reps
  • One Arm Cable Lateral Raise: 3 sets - 16, 12, 8 reps

Day 2: Pull - Lat Focus

  • Lat Pulldown: 4 sets - 16, 12, 8, 16 reps
  • Seated Cable Rows: 3 sets - 16, 12, 8 reps
  • Cable Pull-Over: 4 sets - 16, 12, 8, 16 reps (Last set: static hold in stretched position for 1 sec + 3 partial reps)
  • Alternating Dumbbell Hammer Curls: 2 sets - 16, 12 reps
  • Cable Reverse Fly: 3 sets - 16, 12, 8 reps
  • Alternating Incline Dumbbell Bicep Curls: 2 sets - 16, 12 reps
  • Preacher Curl Machine: 2 sets - 16, 12 reps
  • Hanging Leg Raises: 3 sets - Max reps

Day 3: Legs - Hamstring Focus

  • Leg Extensions: 4 sets - 16, 12, 8, 16 reps
  • Seated Leg Curl: 4 sets - 16, 12, 8, 16 reps (Last set: static hold in stretched position for 1 sec + 3 partial reps)
  • Leg Press: 4 sets - 16, 12, 8, 16 reps
  • Smith Machine RDL: 2 sets - 16, 12 reps
  • Seated Calf Raise Machine: 3 sets - 16, 12, 8 reps

Day 4: Rest

Rest is crucial for muscle growth and recovery. On this day, focus on light activities like stretching or walking to aid recovery. Proper nutrition is also key—ensure you're getting enough protein and staying hydrated. For a more comprehensive rest day strategy and the full 8-day plan, book a call with me!
